About Me

This is where I will be uploading all of my assignments for my web production class. I'm Jennifer Fletcher, a senior majoring in Media Studies. I've always loved creating anything I could. Whether it be physical or digital, I love exploring every creative avenue I can. I'm really enthusiastic about investigating the influence media as a whole has on our day to day lives and learning how to utilize different forms of media to influence others. We all spend a lot of our lives consuming media through online videos, films/television shows, scrolling on various social platforms, ads all around us-- I find it fascinating to study both the effect it has on us, but also how to create it as well. I was involved with filming at Manhattan Neighborhood Network, and it really peaked my interest in this field. I also interned with the Socially Relevant Film Fesitival as the Submissions and Digital Media Coordinator which also furthered my interests.
